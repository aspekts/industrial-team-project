Scope rule

Schema before surface area

The dashboard only exposes fields that can be traced to the current synthetic schema. Unsupported dimensions such as regions or SLA posture do not appear.

Design rule

Cross-source correlation is the product

Useful signals come from linking ATM app errors, hardware faults, Kafka failures, terminal-handler exceptions, runtime pressure, and host metrics into one narrative.

Product rule

Unsupported features stay out

Region tracking, SLA coverage, and team-routing concepts have been removed until those dimensions exist in the pipeline and schema.
